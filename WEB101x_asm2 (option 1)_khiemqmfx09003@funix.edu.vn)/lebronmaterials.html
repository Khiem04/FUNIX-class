3. MATERIALS

The tongue in my Lakers colorway is synthetic suede. While it didn’t chafe as bad as those with the Reflections colorway, it still bugs. The built in air bubbles make the tongue flex weird and it doesn’t really settle down until you lace all the way up. Luckily, the gusseted tongue doesn’t slide side to side. The LeBron 18 is begging for a LeBron 10 style tongue that would better match the nylon heel material.

RATE: 8/10
