4. Lebron 18

About this shoes

18 years of performance basketball shoes built for the most explosive basketball player ever (EVER!!!). LeBron James’ Nike line is typically the flagship for Nike basketball technologies and the Nike LeBron 18 carries on the tradition. The shoe already has a place in history. LeBron won his fourth title overall and first with the Los Angeles Lakers while wearing them in 2020’s NBA Bubble. But despite the hype, the question remains, is the LeBron 18 a great performing basketball shoe? Let’s find out.

1. TRACTION

The traction was very consistent during my time in the Nike LeBron 18. Indoors, it worked wonders. I love the flex grooves as it allowed the rubber to remain in contact with the floor at all times. Outdoors, they’ve held their own as well. Not extremely durable, but effective nonetheless.

RATE: 9/10
