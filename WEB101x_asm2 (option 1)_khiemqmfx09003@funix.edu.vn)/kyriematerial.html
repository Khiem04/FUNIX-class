3. MATERIALS

The Nike Kyrie 7 comes in raw, mesh textiles upper for ventilation and support. The uppers ensure that the whole pair is lightweight, maybe the lightest among the Kyrie series. The heel portion is infused with leather materials that serve as heel counter and additional aesthetics. While the upper materials provide topnotch overall performance and excellent aesthetics, Nike could have infused more premium and innovative materials given that the Nike Kyrie series is becoming one of the most prominent and best-performing signature line available.

RATE: 9/10
